Welcome to Flat Roof Services Kent

Flat Roof Services Kent are specialists in flat roof services in Kent. We always respond quickly and are available 24 hours a day. Flat Roof Services Kent has over 20 years experience in the roofing industry, in that time we have established ourselves as one of Londons leading roofing companies, with satisfied customers throughout the city and the surrounding area. A roof, when repaired properly can leave you with a leak proof roof for many years, when you get us to carry out this work for you.

We will not only fix the problem with your roof, we will look to find the root of the problem, so you can be sure that your roof will not leak again.
Flat Roof Services Kent offer a full range of roofing services from roof repairs, flat roofing, fire walls, valleys, lead work, warm insulated roofs, chimney repairs, roof windows and roof replacements. All our new roof repairs come with a 10 year guarantee
